Our Philosophy

What Is Jeong?

Jeong (정) is a Korean concept with no direct English equivalent. It describes the bond that forms between people — or between a person and something they rely on — through consistent presence, genuine care, and quiet reliability over time.

Jeong is why you still go to the same barber even though a newer shop opened closer to your house. It is why a long-time employee feels irreplaceable even when their job description is ordinary. It is the feeling that builds slowly and holds firmly.

Most AI companies sell capability — features, integrations, hours saved. EBYG Automation leads with relationship. We're building a digital operational presence that becomes genuinely woven into how your business runs.

"We will be here. We will stay involved. We will care about your business the way you care about it."
